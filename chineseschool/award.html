School Committee

Nottingham Chinese School was founded in 1999. In 2012 there are more than 100 pupils in seven classes. The teaching is based on textbooks specially designed for overseas students (ages 4.5-18) studying Chinese. The teaching covers from beginners to GCSE and A level Chinese.

The school runs on Saturday afternoons, from 1:30pm to 2:30pm for school activities (e.g. dancing and caligraphy); from 14:30 to 16:30 for classes, at the Alderman White School Chilwell Lane, Bramcote, NG9 3DU, Nottingham.

History

The idea behind the school started in the begining of Feb 1999 by Dr Qingping Zheng and further effort was put together by Drs Zhang Kefei and Yi Wang with the support from members of the CSSA-NOTT. The school was officially founded on the 21st March 1999 and it is managed by the school committee. Since then the school has been progressing steadily.
