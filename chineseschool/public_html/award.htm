A Best Student Award was set up to encourage students in their Chinese study. For each session, school teachers will record whether pupils have completed the homework assigned, the attendance and the behaviour during class. An overall score will be given at the end of each school year based on the following weight factors:

  • 50% of homework
  • 25% of attendance
  • 25% of classroom behaviour

    • The top two students from each class will be presented with the award and a 5 pounds voucher.

    All students who achieved grade A/A* level in GCSE or A-Level examinations will be awarded with a 5 pounds voucher.
