Textbook / 教科书

The teaching is based on textbooks specially designed for overseas students studying Chinese and with the addition of GCSE and A-Level Chinese content. 中文学校的教学基于专门为海外学生学习汉语而设计的教科书，并增加了GCSE和A-Level汉语内容。

The Zhong Wen textbooks are distributed in the beginning of each academic year. You could also download a pdf version below. 《中文》教科书在每个学年初分发。您也可以在下面点击下载pdf版本。
