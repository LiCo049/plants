Textbook / 教科书

School teaching is based on textbooks specially designed for overseas students studying Chinese and with the addition of GCSE and A-Level Chinese content. 中文学校的教学基于专门为海外学生学习汉语而设计的教科书，并增加了GCSE和A-Level汉语内容。

All textbooks are distributed in the beginning of each academic year. You could also download a pdf version below. 所有的教科书在会在每个学年初分发。您也可以在下面点击下载pdf版本。

Children in the reception classes are learning from You Er Han Yu. These books are designed for 4-6 year-olds children. 小小班的孩子使用教材《幼儿汉语》，这套书适合海外华裔4-6岁的儿童。
