About Us

Nottingham Chinese School was founded in 1999. In 2020 there were more than 300 pupils in seventeen classes. The teaching is based on textbooks specially designed for overseas students (ages 4.5-18) studying Chinese. The teaching covers from beginners to GCSE and A level Chinese.

The school runs on Saturday afternoons, from 12:15 to 14:15, or from 14:30 to 16:30 for language classes. Activity classes (such as dancing, painting, musical instrument and caligraphy) run from 13:30 to 14:30, or 14:30 to 15:30.

Nottingham Chinese Schools is located at the Alderman White School Chilwell Lane, Bramcote, NG9 3DU, Nottingham.

School History

The idea behind the school started in the begining of Feb 1999 by Dr Qingping Zheng and further effort was put together by Drs Zhang Kefei and Yi Wang with the support from members of the CSSA-NOTT. The school was officially founded on the 21st March 1999 and it is managed by the school committee. Since then the school has been progressing steadily.
