'Big Blue' is an upright, herbaceous perennial to 75cm with spiky, highly-divided, silvery-green foliage. The stiff, branched stems are topped with blue, thimble-shaped flowerheads surrounded by a ruff of prickly, silvery-blue bracts.

Eryngium zabelii ‘Big Blue’ is a versatile perennial that produces large spiky steely-blue flowers that rise above the spiny foliage in summer. Perfect for sunny boarders or containers Eryngium zabelii ‘Big Blue’ also works well as an accompaniment to grasses or other architectural plants. Great for attracting bees in summer. Ideal for using in cut flower arrangements or as dried flowers.

Eryngium zabelii ‘Big Blue’ are low maintenance requiring little care. Established plants are drought tolerant. When flowing has finished old stems to ground level to help the plant re-emerge in the following spring.
