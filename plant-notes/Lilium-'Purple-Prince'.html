Giant Hybrid Lily Purple Prince has thick, waxy, 8", deep lavender flowers with green throats. These delightful blooms have a delicate fragrance and are perfect for the back of the border, cut flower arrangements and bouquets. Allow foliage to die back naturally so that the bulb can build up energy for next year. This is the hybridizer's perfect cross between the exotic Orientals and regal trumpet lilies. 16-18 cm bulbs.

A new hybrid of oriental and trumpet lilies which reach a height of up to 2.5m with up to 40, 20cm diameter flowers per bulb. Plant at the back of the border and leave undisturbed. By the third year they will attain their full size. Glossy purple flowers that will brighten up any garden. Giant 18cm+ bulbs supplied.
