Common Name: Lesser celandine

Lesser celandine是林地最早开花的植物之一，多年生草本植物，花期是二月晚期到五月份。花朵有光泽，8-12片花瓣。叶子是油亮的暗绿色，心型，边缘有波纹。林地，篱笆边缘都较常见。

Lesser celandine曾经被用来治疗痔疮，所以也叫做pilewort.

Lesser celandine的花瓣在下雨前会合拢，所以凯特尔人也管它叫做Grian，就是太阳的意思。

Lesser celandine的叶子含有丰富的维生素C，过去被用来治疗败血症。

花中的Lesser celandine就如同鸟中的燕子，二者都是通报春天来临的使者。Celandine 来自希腊语 chelidon 就是燕子的意思，传统上2月21日开花。

Lesser Celandines form showy extensive patches of yellow in hedgerows and woods in early spring. They grow underneath trees, tolerating shade by flowering before the tree foliage grows. The flowers turn to follow the sun during the day, closing in cloudy or cold weather. Flowers usually have 9 petals, but often have 8 or 10, and may have purple streaks on the underside. The leaves are heart-shaped and may be variegated. The word "celandine" derives from the Greek word for swallow (chelidon), as early botanists associated its flowering time with the arrival of the swallows.
