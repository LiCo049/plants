Common Name: Double late tulip 'Copper Image'

Botanical Name: Tulipa 'Copper Image'

Chinese Name: 郁金香




Other references
RHS

A perennial bulb with broadly lance-shaped green leaves. Flowers are double and peony-like, with petals in pastel shades of pinky purple and orange. Stands approximately 35cm high, and flowers in late spring
