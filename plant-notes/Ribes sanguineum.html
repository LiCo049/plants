Common Name: Flowering currant

Botanical Name: Ribes sanguineum

Chinese Name: 醋栗属

Flowering Currant (Ribes Sanguineum) is deciduous and its flowers appear before the leaves. The flowers are formed in showy crimson pendulous highly scented clusters often smothering the plant and are an excellent early nectar for bees. They are followed in Autumn by blue/black currant like fruits with a white bloom which are suitable for us to eat. Its tooth serated leaves have a strong scent when crushed.

Flowering Currant with grow in most soils other than very alkaline, it must however have some moisture, and will perform well in full or partial sun or even light shade. It is suitable for coastal situations.

Flowering currant是一种常见园林落叶灌木，可以长到2米高2米宽。树皮是深灰棕色，树干上有突出的皮孔。适合新手种植。

叶子长2-7厘米，掌状，五叶裂叶; 春天时，幼叶有强烈的树脂香味。

花朵在早春开花，和叶子同时出现，悬挂的总状花序3-7厘米，包含5-30朵花;每朵花的直径为5-10毫米，有五个红色或粉红色的花瓣。

水果是长约1厘米，是深紫色椭圆形的浆果，可食用但据说味道不好。
