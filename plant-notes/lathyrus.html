These Sweet Peas are reliable and easy to grow perennial plants in the prettiest shades. They will climb up any support provided such as an obelisk or trellis and you will be able to cut flowers continually through the summer months. Very little maintenance required, only needing a trim in the autumn and they will shoot again in the spring. Plant in a sunny position and free-draining soil. Fully hardy perennials.

Sweet peas are easy to grow in any fertile soil that drains easily, in full sun. Feed weekly or fortnightly with a liquid fertiliser and ensure a regular supply of flowers by picking or deadheading.

Sweetpea 是英国最受欢迎的花园花卉之一。邻居家去年种了sweet pea/ 香豌豆，几颗种子落到我家院子，发了芽，开了花，柔软的枝条，艳丽的花朵，很别致。

香豌豆英文名 sweet pea, 学名Lathyrus odoratus，是豆科山黧豆属(香豌豆属)下的一种，全属包括130多个品种。

并不是所有的香豌豆都有气味，但是有气味的香豌豆都是名副其实地香。

香豌豆是一年生/多年生攀援草本。

香豌豆虽然也是豆科的，但种子有毒，不能食用。
