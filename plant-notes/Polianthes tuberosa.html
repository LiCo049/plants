Common Name: tuberose, 晚香玉

Botanical Name: Polianthes tuberosa




Other references
RHS
Gardenia
yougarden

Requires a minimum temperature of 15°C, so must be overwintered indoors. Dry off pots at the end of summer when leaves begin to die down, beginning watering and feeding again in spring.

  • Amazingly sweet-scented double flowers, used as the base for many fragrances & perfumes
  • Fabulous grown in a large traditonal pot on the patio, where you can smell them
  • Can be cut as flowers for the vase, and used indoors, will last 10- days or so
  • Flower in August and September at 60-80cm, producing pretty creamy white double flowers
