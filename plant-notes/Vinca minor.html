Botanical Name: Vinca minor

Vinca can be evergreen subshrubs or herbaceous perennials, with simple, paired leaves and solitary, 5-lobed, salver-shaped flowers in the leaf axils

    
Pretty, pale blue or blue-violet flowers from April to September and lance-shaped, dark green leaves. This pretty, prostrate, evergreen shrub is excellent groundcover for the smaller garden. One of our recommended plants, it's less invasive than Vinca major and grows in any well-drained soil in sun or partial shade.

    
Vinca minor is a popular ground cover plant is a smaller version of its vigorous relative, greater periwinkle. Its leaves, flowers and growth rate are about two-thirds those of Vinca major. This restraint makes Vinca minor ‘Illuminations’ more suitable for smaller beds and borders, and also for planting in containers and baskets. It is equally adept at colonising poor, dry, slightly shaded conditions where the creeping and arching stems soon make an attractive carpet of bright green foliage.

    
小蔓长春花花色绚丽，是一种理想的观赏型花卉。从小蔓长春花中可提取长春胺和长春胺衍生物脱水长春胺乙酯（长春西丁）。已经广泛地用于治疗脑血管病变引起的各种症状如偏瘫、失语症和老年性脑退化等。

    
小蔓长春花是低矮蔓延灌木，在地面蔓延的同时在枝茎上生根，会很快侵占大片土地，最高可以爬到40厘米高，但不会盘绕。叶子是常绿的，对生叶序（就是在茎枝的每个节上相对地着生两片叶），2-4.5厘米长，1-2.5厘米宽。

    
小蔓长春花的花朵是长在叶腋上的单花，早春到夏季中期开放，也有部分品种开到七月。花朵大多蓝紫色，（少数栽培品种是浅紫或白色）直径2-3厘米，五片花瓣。
