B. julianae - B. julianae is a dense, upright, spiny, evergreen shrub with rigid, glossy, narrowly elliptic, spiny-toothed, dark green leaves, pale green beneath. Clusters of red-flushed, yellow flowers in late spring are followed by blue-black fruit.

Grows in any soil except waterlogged. A sunny position will encourage fruiting。

Ingestion may cause mild stomach upset. Wear gloves and wash hands after handling。
