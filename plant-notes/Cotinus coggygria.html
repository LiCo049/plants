Propagate by semi-ripe cuttings in summer. Prune if necessary in winter or early spring.

Cotinus is famously known as the ‘smoke bush’, for its hazy plumes of soft pink, summer flowers that really do resemble clouds of soft smoke. However, it’s the oval-shaped foliage of these shrubs that makes a really dramatic statement. There’s a range of cultivars with leaf colour ranging from apple green through to purple, and they all share characteristically brilliant autumn colouring, with a fiery display of yellow, red and deep scarlet. Smoke bushes are mostly quite large shrubs or small trees, many reaching around 4m x 4m, but there are smaller cultivars available. How to grow smoke bush The smoke bush needs well-drained, fertile soil and a spot in a border in full sun or partial shade. Mulch annually with well-rotted manure or leaf mould in spring. It requires little or no pruning.

上周四午休跑步途中我注意到了这棵灌木，卵圆形的叶片，红得鲜艳，枝条是红紫色的，很特别。网上查找一番，原来这是黄栌，又称红叶树/烟树。

黄栌英文名Smoke Tree，Smoke Bush，拉丁学名Cotinus coggygria， 是漆树科（Anacardiaceae）黄栌属（Cotinus）的一种植物。其木材可入药，果实可煮制木蜡。 黄栌属于落叶灌木或乔木，单叶互生，倒卵形，长3～8厘米，宽2.5～6厘米，先端圆或微凹，基部圆或阔楔形，全缘无锯齿。花期4月。果期6月。

黄栌为重要的观赏红叶树种，叶片秋季变红，鲜艳夺目，著名的北京香山红叶就是此树种。在园林中适宜丛植于草坪、土丘或山坡，亦可混植于其它树群尤其是常绿树群中，能为园林增添秋色。

The species[2] and its cultivars 'Royal Purple'[3] and 'Flame'[4] have gained the Royal Horticultural Society's Award of Garden Merit.[4]

黄栌花后久留不落的不孕花的花梗呈粉红色羽毛状在枝头形成似云似雾的梦幻般景观。也是良好的造林树种。

黄栌喜光，也耐半荫；耐寒，耐干旱瘠薄和践行土壤，但不耐水湿。以深厚、肥沃而排水量昊之沙壤土生长最好。生长快；根系发达。萌蘖性强。对二氧化硫有较强抗性。
