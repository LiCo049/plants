BN: Lavendula-angustifolia-Hidcote

RHS
gardenersworld
how to propogate

An excellent dwarf lavender with very dark purple flowers an dnarrow green leaves. Can be used as a low hedge.

Suitable for borders and containers, in sun, on well-drained soils.

Up to 45cm.

Water well before planting and until established. Prune in late summer.

Add flowers to soups, steews and salad. Can be used to make tea.

Choose non-flowered shoots of this year's growth and ensure that they're free from pests and disease.
