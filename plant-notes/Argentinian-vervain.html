How to grow

Cultivation Grow in moist but well-drained or well-drained, moderately fertile soil in full sun. In cold areas, protect with a dry winter mulch

Propagation self seed gently when happy Propagate by seed in pots at 18-21?C in autumn to early spring or let it self-seed

How to care

Pruning Cut down in spring as new growth emerges from the base; deadhead in autumn if seed is not required
