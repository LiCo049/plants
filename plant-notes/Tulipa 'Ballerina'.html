Common Name: Tulip 'Ballerina'

Botanical Name: Tulipa 'Ballerina'

Chinese Name: 中文名




Other references

RHS
sarahraven
Gardenia


'Ballerina' is a perennial bulb to 60cm in height, with grey-green foliage and yellow flowers, prominently flamed with red outside, and red within, in late spring。

Plant bulbs in autumn - not too early, November is best. Excellent in swathes through borders or packed into large containers.
